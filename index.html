❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my business?

Go to Account → Register Business and fill out the required information. Your business will be reviewed by our admin team.

How do I find businesses near me?

Allow location access when prompted, then browse the home screen to see nearby businesses. You can also search by category.

How do I contact a business?

Tap on any business to view their contact details including phone, WhatsApp, and Instagram.

How much does it cost to register a business?

Business registration is currently FREE during our first phase! We're building our community and supporting local entrepreneurs at no cost.
